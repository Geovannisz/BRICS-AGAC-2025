Registration

Join us for BRICS-AGAC 2025 in João Pessoa!

Registration Fees

All participants are required to register. The registration fee includes access to all scientific sessions, conference materials, coffee breaks, welcome reception, and certificate of participation.

Participation in the 5th BRICS-AGAC Symposium is free of charge for all attendees.

Registration Process

To register for BRICS-AGAC 2025, please complete the external registration form. During registration, you will be required to submit your abstract. You will also have the option to express interest in the technical visit to the BINGO Telescope and to request an invitation letter for visa purposes.
