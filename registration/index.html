Mandatory Steps After Payment

To confirm your registration, please follow these steps carefully:

  1. Send Proof of Payment: After payment, you **must** email the receipt to bricsagac5@secties.pb.gov.br .
  2. Specify Email Subject: Use the subject line: "Registration Payment - [Your Full Name]".
  3. Await Confirmation: Your registration is only guaranteed after our team verifies your payment. A confirmation email will be sent to you.

Registration Process

To register for BRICS-AGAC 2025, please complete the external registration form. During registration, you will be required to submit your abstract. You will also have the option to express interest in the technical visit to the BINGO Telescope and to request an invitation letter for visa purposes.
