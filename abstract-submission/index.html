Abstract Submission

Share your research at BRICS-AGAC 2025.

Call for Abstracts

We invite submissions for oral and poster presentations for the 5th BRICS-AGAC Symposium. This is an excellent opportunity to present your work to an international audience of experts and peers in Gravity, Astrophysics, and Cosmology.

Guidelines for Submission

  • Abstracts must be written in English.
  • The abstract body should not exceed 300 words .
  • Please indicate your preference for oral or poster presentation (the scientific committee will make the final decision).
  • Blind Submission: To ensure an unbiased selection process, please do not include your name or any identifying information in the abstract text. Submissions containing author names or affiliations will not be considered.
  • Submissions should be made via the online submission portal, as indicated below.

Scientific Themes/Topics

Abstracts are welcome in, but not limited to, the following areas:

  • Early Universe Cosmology
  • Inflationary Models
  • Dark Matter & Dark Energy
  • Large Scale Structure
  • Cosmic Microwave Background
  • Gravitational Waves: Theory & Observation
  • Black Hole Physics & Astrophysics
  • Neutron Stars & Compact Objects
  • Modified Theories of Gravity
  • Quantum Gravity & Quantum Cosmology
  • Numerical Relativity & Simulations
  • Multi-messenger Astrophysics

Important Dates for Abstract Submission

  • Abstract Submission Deadline: November 14, 2025

Note: Official acceptance letters will be provided upon request for participants who require them for visa applications or funding purposes.

How to Submit

Abstracts must be submitted directly via the official registration form. The submission is a required step to complete your registration.
