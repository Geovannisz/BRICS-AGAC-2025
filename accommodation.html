Accommodation in João Pessoa

Find a comfortable place to stay during the conference.

We recommend that hotel reservations be made after your registration is confirmed to ensure negotiated rates and better prices. The city is a tourist destination and the event period coincides with the high holiday season, so we suggest that reservations be made in advance to ensure availability at the hotels of your choice.

Note: The conference organizers do not have block bookings with these hotels unless specified. Please book directly with the hotel or through your preferred booking platform. Mentioning BRICS-AGAC 2025 *might* provide a conference rate at some establishments, but this is not guaranteed.

Additional Tips for Booking

  • Consider proximity to the conference venue and public transport.
  • Check reviews on platforms like Booking.com, TripAdvisor, or Google Maps.
  • Look for amenities that are important to you (e.g., Wi-Fi, breakfast included, gym).
  • Airbnb and other short-term rental options are also available in João Pessoa.
