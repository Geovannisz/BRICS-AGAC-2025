Registration

Join us for BRICS-AGAC 2025 in João Pessoa!

Registration Fees

All participants are required to register. The registration fee includes access to all scientific sessions, conference materials, coffee breaks, welcome reception, and certificate of participation.

Category Fee
Regular Participant (Professors/Researchers) $100 USD
Post-doctoral Participant $60 USD
Student Participant (Proof of status required) $20 USD

Note: All registration fees must be paid in-person on the first day of the event during credential pickup.

*Please be aware that online payment processing is currently under construction. We will notify all registered participants once it is operational.

Registration Process

To register for BRICS-AGAC 2025, please complete the external registration form. During registration, you will be required to submit your abstract. You will also have the option to express interest in the technical visit to the BINGO Telescope and to request an invitation letter for visa purposes.

REGISTER NOW (via Google Forms)

Cancellation Policy

For registration cancellations, a 40% refund of the paid fee is applicable. Cancellation requests must be sent to the official event email address before the event start date.

Important Notes:

  • Student registration requires a valid student ID or a letter from your institution.
  • For any registration queries, please contact bricsagac5@secties.pb.gov.br.
