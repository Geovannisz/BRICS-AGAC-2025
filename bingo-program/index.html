BINGO Program

Exclusive tour to the BINGO Telescope.

Technical Tour: BINGO Telescope

Join us for an exclusive two-day technical tour to the BINGO Telescope construction site in Aguiar, located in the hinterlands of Paraíba. This is a unique opportunity to witness the development of a next-generation instrument in global cosmology.

Please note: The technical visit to the BINGO telescope is exclusively for registered event participants and is not open to the general public.

Transportation

Round Trip Bus

Per person

A 50-seat bus will be provided. Please note: only 35 seats are available for general participants, allocated on a first-come, first-served basis according to registration order. The remaining 15 seats are reserved for the organizing committee, speakers, and special guests. A waiting list will be created for exceeding registrations.

Confirmation Required

Participants who expressed interest in the BINGO technical visit during registration must confirm their attendance in person during the event check-in on the first day (December 9th). Failure to confirm may result in your seat being offered to someone on the waiting list.

Participants on the waiting list will be notified via email after the first day if seats become available due to cancellations or no-shows.
